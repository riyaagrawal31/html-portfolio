The Best Movies According to Riya

My top 3 movies of all time.

Phir Hera Peri

This OG movie is my favourite as it never ever fails to make me laugh, no matter the number of times I watch it!

Gumraah

This movie had me on the edge of my seat from the beginning till the end. The story was really intriguing. Acting- TOP NOTCH. A perfect suspence thriller, like i love to watch.

CTRL

The perfect movie to watch being a student on the IT field. Loved the idea of this movie based on cyber security. Sci-fi, but cooler!
